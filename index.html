Social Media Marketing & Management

What is Social Media Management?
Social Media Management is the management, monitoring, contributing to and guiding the social media presence of a brand, product, individual or corporation.

Why is Social Media Management Important?
There are so many opportunities to learn more about your customers and be able to engage in meaningful conversations about your business. Learning about your customers has never been easier with the use of tools such as Facebook and Instagram Insights, obtaining the demographics of your online audience allows for businesses be able to better understand, connect with and cater to the needs of their audience.

Why Social Media Marketing?
With 3.196 billion people using social media and 11 new people starting to use social media every second, this means that there are about 1 million people every day who are moving forward with technology and being exposed to new brands and businesses. Social Media Marketing is important because it allows for an increase in brand recognition, it is effective and inexpensive and it generates more leads/sales.
